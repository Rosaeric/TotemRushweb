FAQs

I purchased but didn't receive my rank/key.

Most purchases apply within 10 minutes. Make sure you were online at checkout and rejoin the server. If it still hasn't arrived after 30 minutes, submit a billing request with your order ID and Minecraft username.

How do I appeal a punishment?

Appeals are handled via our support request form. Provide your IGN, approximate date, and why you believe the action should be reviewed.

How do I report a bug or player?

Use the Bug Reports or Player Reports categories in our support request form. Include steps to reproduce, screenshots, or video if possible.
